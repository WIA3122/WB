World Boss (written by EQ_tung0312)

This was my info look up at Official Server Pokemeow. If anything is wrong or if you have any suggestions to add, please tell me on Discord at EQ_tung0312(Last update : 6/27/2024 18:57 (GMT+7))

This feature was just released about 3-4 days ago as of my writing. If there are any issues, please let me know so I can fix them. Thank you.

This feature is free to play for all players with only one requirement: you need to be on the Official Server to register each time you play.

World boss has two ways to spawn:

  • By vote in top.gg for Pokemeow: When it has enough votes (1000 votes), it will spawn if there's no boss spawn before it within 1 hour.
  • By staff and admin spawn: Each staff in the Official Server has one spawn per day (reset at 11 AM (GMT+7) ). Admins can spawn multiple times (Their game,their rule xD) .
  • After the boss spawns, you have 2 min to register . If you don't register in time, you can't fight the boss.

HOW TO PLAY

You can check when the boss spawns or not by using command ;worldboss / ;wb or check in the channel named Worldboss in the Official Server.

After the boss spawns, you can register by using command ;wb / ;worldboss in any channel in the Official Server and click in the register buttom below (have 2 buttom if you have fight that boss once).

*Note: Once you register, you can't change the position of the team, and in the boss fight, you can't swap Pokémon out, so be careful. Also, you can't change Pokémon until the next boss.

*NOTE : You alway move first and the recharge , charge move dont need to recharge , charge so use you stongest move for max damage !!!

To fight the boss, use command ;wb fight / ;worldboss fight in any channel in any server that has Pokemeow.

You need to deal at least 0.1% of the boss's health to get a reward .The more damage you do the more reward you get . The top 1 damage will get a bonus reward.

The rewards include: Coins (get it every time), lootbox, rare candy, rare/superrare/legend/shiny box, evolution egg, double exp ticket, etc.

After the boss dies, you can claim the reward by using command ;wb claim / ;worldboss claim in any server that have Pokemeow bot (if that channel can use commands).

There are some special rewards including: excl shiny/golden mon, excl item, Gmax mon or even Shiny Gmax mon (Only shiny Gmax mon have a chance to drop Shiny Gmax mon).

After the boss dies, the next boss will spawn after 1 hour (if it has enough votes ) or if it is spawned by admin or staff (no cooldown).

SOME FUN FACTS ABOUT WORLD BOSS

Bosses only have a small chance to attack your Pokémon (higher if you use GOLDEN MON).

Bosses are immune to status effects and accuracy effects.

It's quite easy to deal enough damage to get a reward. Remember, even if you're not in the Top 1 damage, you still have a chance to get exclusive rewards (Top 1 has a higher chance), so why not give it a try? xD
