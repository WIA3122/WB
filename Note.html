USEFUL POKEMON FOR WORLD BOSS

Muk

Moves:

  • Acid-spray
  • Screech
  • Memento

How to Use:

Muk have every debuff need to reduce enemy defense (Both sp def and def) . Use Acid-spray if you main damage is sp atk and Screech if atk . After done debuff (6 stage) then use memento to reduce enemy atk

  • Memento

How to Use:

Just use memento to reduce enemy atk easy enough xD

Mega-Mewtwo-y

Moves:

  • Amnesia
  • Nasty-plot
  • Dream-eater
  • Future-sight

How to Use:

Mega-Mewtwo-y(or most call MMY) is a a true carry. After 2 debuff mon we will set up for MMY start with 2 Amnesia for tank enough to not dead when set up then nasty-plot 2-3 times for maximun damage . Spam future-sight and ALWAYS use dream-eater when low hp.

Mega-Mewtwo-x

Moves:

  • Amnesia
  • Power-Up-Punch
  • Drain-Punch
  • Focus-Punch

How to Use:

Like MMY , Mega-Mewtwo-x (or call MMX) is super carry . As MMY you still need 2 Amnesia to tank enough then spam power up punch 4-6 times for maximun damage. Then spam Focus-Punch and ALWAY USE Drain-Punch when lower than 200 hp
